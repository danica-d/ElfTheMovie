ELF

directed by Jon Favreau

Elf is a classic American Christmas comedy film directed by Jon Favreau. It is about one of Santa's elves, Buddy (Will Ferrell), who spends his childhood with fellow elves, but goes on a journey to realize his true identity as a human. He does so by traveling to New York City in search of a his biological father (James Caan) while spreading Christmas joy along the way.
